My Story

From Makerere to Stanford: Building Africa's Digital Security

Cybersecurity graduate with First-Class Honors from Makerere University, currently enrolled in Stanford University's Advanced Cybersecurity Program, and ISACA member building Africa's digital security infrastructure. As Founder of MildSec, I help organisations navigate the convergence of cybersecurity, AI governance, and digital assets—three of the fastest-evolving risk landscapes on the continent. My work spans IT audit, GRC frameworks, and responsible AI policy, with a focus on making critical infrastructure secure and resilient. What drives me: Africa's digital economy needs trusted, locally-informed governance. I'm committed to ensuring women aren't just participants but leaders in shaping secure, ethical technology across the region.
